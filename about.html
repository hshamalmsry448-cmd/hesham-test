Publishing Policy

  • Publication is limited to cultural, social, artistic, and sports events in Damascus.
  • All information must be accurate and complete (date, location, details).
  • Content that is illegal, offensive, or discriminatory is not allowed.
  • The website administration reserves the right to edit or remove any material that does not comply with this policy.
  • The accuracy of the published information is the responsibility of the event organizer.
